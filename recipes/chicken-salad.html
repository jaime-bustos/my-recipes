Chicken Salad


Chicken salad is a popular dish that has many uses. It can be used in sandwiches, salads, wraps, crackers, and even by itself.

Ingredients

  • shredded cooked chicken
  • mayonaise
  • boiled egg
  • salt
  • pepper
  • relish
  • paprika
  • lime

Steps

  1. Combine shredded chicken and spoonful of mayonaise in a bowl.
  2. Dice boiled egg to preferred-sized pieces.
  3. Combine in the same bowl: diced egg, salt, pepper, relish, and paprika.
  4. Squeeze lime into the bowl.
  5. Mix until ingredients are evenly distributed.
